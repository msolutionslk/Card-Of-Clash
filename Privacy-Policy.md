# Privacy Policy for Card Of Clash

**Last Updated:** March 23, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Card Of Clash**.  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected through our mobile game.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game, including multiplayer features and rewarded advertisements.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### 🔹 Device & Technical Information
To provide a stable multiplayer experience and maintain game sessions, we collect:
- Device ID (or unique identifier)
- Device model and brand
- Operating system and version
- Browser or app version
- IP address
- Network information

### 🔹 Gameplay Information
- Player scores and rankings  
- Game session data  
- Multiplayer room/session identifiers  
- In-game progress and credits  

### 🔹 Advertising Data
When you interact with rewarded ads (e.g., earning credits), third-party providers (such as Google AdMob) may collect:
- Advertising ID  
- Interaction with ads  
- Device and usage data  

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- 🎮 Create and manage **isolated multiplayer game rooms**
- 🧠 Identify devices to maintain **player sessions and fairness**
- 🏆 Track **scores, rankings, and game progress**
- ⚙️ Improve game performance and fix issues
- 📊 Analyze usage to enhance gameplay experience
- 💰 Provide **rewarded ads** and grant in-game credits

---

## 4. Multiplayer Functionality

Card Of Clash includes real-time multiplayer features.  
To enable this:

- Devices are uniquely identified (not personally identified)
- Players are matched into game rooms
- Game state and progress are synchronized securely

We do **not** collect personal identity information like name, email, or phone number unless explicitly provided.

---

## 5. Advertising (Rewarded Ads)

We use **Google AdMob** or similar advertising networks to serve rewarded ads.

- Users may choose to watch ads in exchange for in-game rewards
- These providers may collect and process data under their own privacy policies

👉 Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
